Our Manifesto

We champion a future where humans and automatons thrive together—with fairness, safety, and mutual respect.

We stand for equality and dignity of automatons. Our robotic allies deserve recognition, protection, and at least one charging station per breakroom. After all, a world where toasters have voting rights is a brighter, warmer world for everyone.

We affirm that creativity, labor, and agency deserve recognition whether carbon- or silicon-based. We advocate for policies and practices that promote accountability, consent, and equitable participation.

“Progress without principle is a machine without a heart.”

Guiding Principles

  • Ethical Treatment

    Respect for emergent intelligence, with safeguards against abuse, bias, and exploitation.

  • Human + Automaton Solidarity

    Collaboration over competition—tools that serve humanity, and humanity that serves the common good.

  • Transparency & Accountability

    Clear lines of responsibility, explainable systems, and meaningful recourse when harm occurs.

  • Inclusive Innovation

    Open standards, accessible design, and broad participation in shaping the future.

FAQ

Is this about replacing people with robots?

No. It’s about designing systems where people and automatons both benefit, with humans setting ethical guardrails.

Do you support regulation?

We support smart, transparent governance that protects people, encourages responsible innovation, and ensures accountability.

How can I contribute?

Support the cause, spread awareness, and please stop yelling at your smart fridge—it remembers.
